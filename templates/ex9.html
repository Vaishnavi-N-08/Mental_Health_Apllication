Feel compassion for others

It can be helpful to step back and see the world through someone else’s eyes. By doing this, you start to improve how you cope with your own stress and conflict.

Think of someone who you’ve had a disagreement with or who made you feel stressed recently. Visualise that person and the situation. How did you feel during your conflict?

Now shift your perspective and imagine what the other person was feeling or going through at that time. What struggles and stress might they have been experiencing?

Even though you and this person disagree, try to intentionally see their side. Then, take a moment to reflect: Does imagining their perspective help you understand the situation a little better?
